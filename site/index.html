07 · SOURCES & STANCE

A cultural archive, not a proof engine.

WHAT THIS IS

A structured record of how communities tell timeline-shift stories: who reported what, when, in what kind of source, and with what confidence. Official science facts sit on their own shelf, always cited.

WHAT IT NEVER CLAIMS

That CERN, the LHC, or particle physics proves timeline manipulation. No scientific, medical, or prophetic claims. No harassment of researchers, believers, or skeptics. One source family is summarized without naming its teacher — by design.

EVIDENCE GRADES
CONTRIBUTE A REPORT

Open an issue with the report template: what you experienced or found, when, where it was posted, and a link if one exists. Curators grade it, tag motifs, and file it. Excerpts stay short; summaries and URLs are preferred over copies.

Corrections welcome — especially official sources for anything flagged ⚑ needs source.
