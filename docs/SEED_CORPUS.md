# Seed Corpus

This file turns the provided research note into a first project corpus. It is
not final data. Each item should later become JSON with source links, status,
and review flags.

## Core premise

The seed narrative connects four layers:

1. Official high-energy physics milestones.
2. Community stories about Mandela Effects and timeline shifts.
3. Symbolic/alchemical motifs about broken time, light, serpents, and inner
   calibration.
4. Personal meaning-making around choosing or anchoring more harmonious
   timelines.

The project should preserve this internal logic while labeling it as symbolic
and speculative.

## Initial story threads

### Collider as threshold

Belief frame:

The collider becomes a symbol of humanity trying to touch the origin point of
matter from the outside. In the story logic, extreme collision mirrors an
attempt to reach creation through force.

Neutral frame:

The corpus records how communities turn high-energy physics milestones into
symbols of threshold, origin, and reality instability.

Skeptical frame:

The LHC is a particle accelerator. Official sources describe physics research,
maintenance, and upgrades, not timeline manipulation.

Motifs:

- `collider`
- `origin-search`
- `threshold`
- `machine-mirror`

### Machine silence calibration

Belief frame:

When the machine becomes silent, the inner field can be calibrated. The pause is
read as a window for reflection, music, ritual, and heart-based alignment.

Neutral frame:

The report cluster links the 2026 shutdown to a symbolic "pause" motif.

Motifs:

- `machine-silence`
- `calibration`
- `heart-collider`
- `music`

## Initial motif list

- `machine-silence`: shutdown, pause, or quiet machine interpreted as a window.
- `rainbow-bridge`: restored connection between matter, spirit, color, and
  dimensions.
- `lost-ion`: missing particle or lost element as a symbolic cause of
  fragmentation.
- `serpent-time`: time as serpent, loop, wound, or chaos force.
- `heart-collider`: consciousness as the nonviolent inner collider.
- `timeline-bleed`: overlap between remembered and current versions of reality.
- `mandela-residue`: artifacts or memories treated as traces of another branch.
- `simulation-glitch`: reality experienced as rendered, patched, or overwritten.
- `machine-mirror`: external scientific machine as mirror of inner process.
- `poison-remedy`: harmful force transformed into medicine.
- `apophis-window`: 2029 serpent/chaos symbolic anchor.
- `silicon-mirror`: mineral and computation as a consciousness mirror.
