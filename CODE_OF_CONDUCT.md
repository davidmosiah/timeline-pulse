# Code of Conduct

Timeline Pulse sits in an unusual spot: it archives beliefs some contributors
hold sincerely and others consider obviously false. Both kinds of people are
welcome here, and both are protected by the same rule:

**Critique claims, grade evidence, never attack people.**

That means, concretely:

- No mocking believers for believing, or skeptics for doubting.
- No harassment of researchers, institutions, posters, or public figures —
  including "go ask them" brigading.
- No armchair diagnoses of people who report experiences.
- No doxxing; respect the `author_policy` field (public / redacted / omitted).
- Disputes about content are settled by the source policy
  ([docs/SOURCE_POLICY.md](docs/SOURCE_POLICY.md)), not by volume.

Everything in the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
applies as the baseline: be respectful, no harassment, no trolling, no
sexualized or discriminatory conduct, in all project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues to `mosiahdavid@gmail.com` (subject
`[timeline-pulse conduct]`) or through a
[safety / policy issue](../../issues/new?template=safety-copyright.yml) if
public. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and ban
contributors who violate this document. Reports are handled confidentially.
